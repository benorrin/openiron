# OpenIron - Self-Hosted Gym Tracker

A lightweight, self-hosted web application for tracking workouts, logging body metrics, and monitoring fitness progress. Perfect for homelab enthusiasts who want full control over their fitness data.

## What is OpenIron?

OpenIron is a comprehensive gym tracking platform that you can run on your own server. Track your workouts, log body measurements, upload progress photos, and analyze your fitness journey - all without sharing your data with third-party services.

I created OpenIron when I became frustrated with the lack of decent fitness tracking software available for gym users. Most apps were focused on cardio/running, required subscriptions, or didn't have the features I wanted. So I created my own - one that focuses primarily on weightlifting/gym activities (while supporting other exercises too), and is completely open source and self-hosted.
